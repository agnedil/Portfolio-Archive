=======================  First Aid Measures  =======================

First Aid:EYE: FLUSH EYES WITH LOTS OF RUNNING WATER FOR 15 MINUTES.
    GET MEDICAL ATTENTION IMMEDIATELY. SKIN: WASH SKIN THOROUGHLY WITH
    SOAP AND WATER. IF IRRITATION PERSISTS, GET MEDICAL ATTENTION.
    INHALATION:  REMOVE VICTIM TO FRESH AIR. IF SYMPTOMS PERSIST, GET
    MEDICAL ATTENTION. INGESTION: DRINK PLENTY OF WATER. GET MEDICAL
    ATTENTION IMMEDIATELY.
