=======================  First Aid Measures  =======================

First Aid:IF SWALLOWED: DRINK 1 OR 2 GLASSES OF WATER & INDUCE
    VOMITING. DON'T INDUCE VOMITING OR GIVE ANYTHING TO AN UNCONSCIOUS
    PERSON. IF INHALED: KEEP PATIENT AT REST. SKIN: WASH W/SOAP &
    WATER. EYES: RINSE	W/WATER. NOTE TO PHYSICIAN: PRODUCT CONTAINS
    CARBAMATE INSECTICIDE. IN CASE OF OVEREXPOSURE, 2 MG ATROPINE
    SULFATE (EQUIVALENT TO 1/30 GRAIN ADULT DOSE) SHOULD BE GIVEN &
    REPEATED AS NECESSARY (SUPP)

=====================  Fire Fighting Measures  =====================

Extinguishing Media:DRY CHEMICAL, FOAM OR WATER
Fire Fighting Procedures:WEAR SCBA. AVOID INHALATION OF FUMES/DUST.
    EVACUATE AREA.
Unusual Fire/Explosion Hazard:WHEN HEATED TO DECOMPOSITION (>280F)
    BENDIOCARB GIVES OFF ISOCYANATE FUMES, WHICH ARE RAPIDLY ELIMINATED
    BY FIRE & WATER.

==================  Accidental Release Measures  ==================

Spill Release Procedures:ISOLATE AREA. SWEEP UP OR VACUUM (PREFERRED) &
    PUT IN SEALED CONTAINER. DON'T RAISE DUST. WEAR PROTECTIVE
    CLOTHING. WASH AREA W/WATER AFTER CLEAN-UP. KEEP WASHINGS OUT OF
    WATER COURSES & SEWERS.

======================	Handling and Storage  ======================

Handling and Storage Precautions:DON'T CONTAMINATE FEED OR FOOD
    PRODUCTS OR FOOD PREPARATION SURFACES, DISHES, KITCHEN UTENSILS AND
    FOOD CONTAINERS. STORE IN COOL, DRY AREA.
Other Precautions:THIS PRODUCT IS TOXIC TO FISH, BIRDS, BEES, & OTHER
    WILDLIFE. KEEP AWAY FROM CHILDREN. WASH CONTAMINATED CLOTHING
    BEFORE RE-USE. DON'T BREATHE DUST & AVOID CONTACT W/EYES, SKIN &
    CLOTHING.
