=====================  Hazards Identification  =====================

Reports of Carcinogenicity:NTP:NO    IARC:NO	OSHA:NO
Health Hazards Acute and Chronic:LONG TERM OVEREXPOSURE OF SOLVENTS IS
    SUSPECTED OF CAUSING LIVER, KIDNEY, LUNG AND CENTRAL NERVOUS SYSTEM
    DAMAGE.  INTENTIONAL MISUSE BY DELIBERATELY CONCENTRATING AND
    INHALING SOLVENTS MAY BE HARMFUL  OR FATAL.
Effects of Overexposure:INHALATION: EXCESSIVE INHALATION OF VAPORS CAN
    CAUSE NASAL AND RESPIRATORY IRRITATION.  MAY CAUSE DIZZINESS,
    WEAKNESS, FATIGUE, NAUSEA, HEADACHE, POSSIBLE UNCONSCIOUSNESS AND
    EVEN ASPHYXIATION. SKIN:  CAN CAUSE REDNESS, DRYNESS, OR RASH. EYE:
    CAN CAUSE IRRITATION, REDNESS, TEARING, AND BLURRED VISION. SKIN
    ABSORPTION: PROLONGED CONTACT CAN CAUSE IRRITATION, DRY SKIN,
    CRACKS, AND DERMATITIS. INGESTI ON: MAY CAUSE VOMITING, NAUSEA,
    DIARRHEA, AND GASTROINTESTINAL IRRITATION.
Medical Cond Aggravated by Exposure:RESPIRATORY AND SKIN SENSITIVITY.

=======================  First Aid Measures  =======================

First Aid:INHALATION: MOVE VICTIM TO FRESH AIR, AID BREATHING, CALL A
    PHYSICIAN.	EYE: FLUSH EYES IMMEDIATELY WITH LARGE AMOUNTS OF WATER
    FOR AT LEAST 15 MINS. AND CALL A PHYSICIAN.  SKIN: REMOVE
    CONTAMINATED C LOTHING, WASH AREA IMMEDIATELY WITH SOAP AND WATER. 
    SEE PHYSICIAN IF IRRITATION PERSISTS.  INGESTION: DRINK 1 OR 2
    GLASSES OF WATER TO DILUTE.  DO NOT INDUCE VOMITING.  CONSULT A
    PHYSICIAN OR POISON  CONTROL CENTER IMMEDIATELY.

==================  Accidental Release Measures  ==================

Spill Release Procedures:SOAK UP SPILLS WITH INERT MATERIAL, I.E.,
    SAND, AND SCRAPE UP WITH NON-SPARKING TOOLS.  PUT IN COVERED METAL
    CONTAINER FOR DISPOSAL.

======================	Handling and Storage  ======================

Handling and Storage Precautions:STORE IN TIGHTLY CLOSED CONTAINERS.
    KEEP AWAY FROM SPARKS, EXCESSIVE HEAT OR OPEN FLAMES. TRANSFER
    BETWEEN CONTAINERS THAT HAVE BEEN ELECTRICALLY GROUNDED. SEE OSHA,
    29 CFR, PART 1910. WET DOWN ALL US ED WIPING RAGS AND USE A COVERED
    METAL CONTAINER TO PREVENT SPONTANEOUS COMBUSTION.
Other Precautions:DO NOT TAKE INTERNALLY, CAN NOT BE MADE NONPOISONOUS.
    AVOID INHALATION WITH SKIN CONTACT. RELEASE PRESSURE ON CONTAINER
    SLOWLY. STORE AND USE IN VENTILATED AREAS. WASH CONTAMINATED
    CLOTHING BEFORE REU SE. DO NOT DRAIN, SLIDE, PUNCTURE, OR REUSE
    CONTAINERS. DO NOT USE PRESSURE TO EMPTY CONTAINERS. DO  NOT SPRAY
    LACQUER.
