=====================  Hazards Identification  =====================

Routes of Entry: Inhalation:YES  Skin:YES  Ingestion:YES
Effects of Overexposure:INHALATION:IRRITATION OF RESPIRATORY
    TRACT,COUGHING,HEADACHE,DIZZINESS,DROWSINESS,NAUSEA,INCOORDINATED
    MOVEMENTS.SKIN:EXCESSIVE SKIN CONTACT MAY CAUSE DRYING AND CRACKING
    OF THE SKIN AND RESULT IN DER MATITIS.EYES:CONTACT WITH EYES WILL
    CAUSE IRRITATION AND DEFATTING OF TISSUE.

=======================  First Aid Measures  =======================

First Aid:INHALATION:REMOVE TO FRESH AIR IF EXPOSED TO EXCESS
    CONCENTRATIONS OF VAPOR.SEEK MEDICAL ATTENTION IF SYMPTOMS
    PERSIST.SKIN AND EYES:FLUSH ANY SKIN OR EYE CONTACT WITH PLENTY OF
    WATER.ADDITIONALLY WIT H SKIN CONTACT,WASH W/SOAP AND WATER.CONTACT
    PHYSICIAN IF IRRITATION PERSISTS.INGESTION:DO NOT INDUCE
    VOMITING.CALL POISON CONTROL CENTER OR PHYSICIAN FOR GUIDANCE.

=====================  Fire Fighting Measures  =====================

Fire Fighting Procedures:PROTECT FIRE FIGHTERS FROM TOXIC PRODUCTS OF

==================  Accidental Release Measures  ==================

Spill Release Procedures:EXTINGUISH OPEN FLAMES AND HEAT-PRODUCING
    EQUIPMENT.VENTILATE AREA OF SPILL OR LEAK.FOR EXPOSURE ABOVE
    TLV,WEAR APPROVED RESPIRATORY EQUIPMENT.CONTAIN SPILL,PREVENTING IT
    FROM ENTERING SEWER LINES OR  WATERWAYS.PICK UP BY SUCTIONING OR
    W/ABSORBENT.
