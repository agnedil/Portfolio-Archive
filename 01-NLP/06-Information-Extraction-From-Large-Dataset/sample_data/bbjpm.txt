=======================  First Aid Measures  =======================

First Aid:INHALATION: REMOVE TO FRESH AIR. IF BREATHING STOPS, GIVE
    ARTIFICIAL RESPIRATION. KEEP WARM/AT REST. SKIN: REMOVE
    CONTAMINATED CLOTHING/SHOES IMMEDIATELY. WASH SKIN W/SOAP OR MILD
    DETERGENT & PLENTY O F WATER UNTIL NO EVIDENCE OF CHEMICAL REMAINS 
    15-20 MINS. EYES: FLUSH W/WATER OR NORMAL SALINE, LIFTING UPPER &
    LOWER LIDS, UNTIL NO EVIDENCE OF CHEMICAL REMAINS  15-20. SEE SUPP.
