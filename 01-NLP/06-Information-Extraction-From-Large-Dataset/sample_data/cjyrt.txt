=======================  First Aid Measures  =======================

First Aid:SWALLOWING: IF MATERIAL IS SWALLOWED, DO NOT INDUCE VOMITING,
    INSTEAD DRINK PLENTY OF WATER AND CONTACT PHYSICIAN. SKIN: WASH
    THOROUGHLY WITH SOAP AND WATER. INHALATION: NONE. EYES: FLUSH WITH
    WATER 1 5 MINUTES. IF IRRITATION CONTINUES, CONTACT PHYSICIAN.
