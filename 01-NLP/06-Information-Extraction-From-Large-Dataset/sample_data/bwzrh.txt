=======================  First Aid Measures  =======================

First Aid:EYES-FLUSH WITH LOTS OF WATER FOR 15 MINUTES. CONTACT A
    PHYSICIAN. SKIN-WASH WITH SOAP & WATER. REMOVE CONTAMINATED
    CLOTHES. INHALED-REMOVE TO FRESH AIR. GIVE OXYGEN IF BREATHING
    DIFFICULT. CONSULT A  PHYSICIAN IMMEDIATELY. INGESTION-CONTACT A
    PHYSICIAN IMMEDIATELY.

=====================  Fire Fighting Measures  =====================

Autoignition Temp:Autoignition Temp Text:>500F
Extinguishing Media:FLOOD WITH WATER; DO NOT SMOTHER WITH BLANKET OR
    DRY CHEMCIAL AS PERCHLORATES SUPPLY THEIR OWN OXYGEN TO FEED A
    FIRE.
Fire Fighting Procedures:NONE SPECIFIED BY MANUFACTURER. DLA-HMIS: WEAR
    SELF-CONTAINED BREATHING APPARATUS AND FULL PROTECTIVE GEAR.
Unusual Fire/Explosion Hazard:NONE SPECIFIED BY MANUFACTURER. DLA-HMIS:
    SEVERE EXPLOSION HAZARD.

==================  Accidental Release Measures  ==================

Spill Release Procedures:SWEEP UP CAREFULLY. AVOID DUST FORMATION USING
    A STATIC FREE BROOM AND A NON-SPARKING CONTAINER. DO NOT USE
    COMPRESSED AIR TO BLOW DOWN AREA.
Neutralizing Agent:NONE SPECIFIED BY MANUFACTURER.

======================	Handling and Storage  ======================

Handling and Storage Precautions:STORE IN A DRY TEMPERATURE CONTROLLED
    OVEN. KEEP AWAY FROM SOURCES OF IGNITION & STRONG OXIDIZING AGENTS.
    KEEP CONTAINERS TIGHTLY CLOSED.
Other Precautions:STORAGE TEMPERATURE=110F. AVOID STATIC DISCHARGES.
    GROUND STRAP OR CONDUCTIVE FOOTWEAR FOR ALL PERSONNEL WORKING WITH
    THIS MATERIAL. GLASS VIALS MAY BE COVERED WITH ALUMINUM FOIL.
