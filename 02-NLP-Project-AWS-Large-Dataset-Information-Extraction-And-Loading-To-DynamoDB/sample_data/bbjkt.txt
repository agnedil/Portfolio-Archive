=======================  First Aid Measures  =======================

First Aid:INHALATION: REMOVE TO FRESH AIR. SUPPORT BREATHING IF
    NECESSARY. EYES: REMOVE CONTACT LENSES. FLUSH W/WATER FOR 15 MINS.
    SKIN: PROMPTLY WASH EXPOSED AREA W/MILD SOAP & WARM WATER. DON'T
    USE HARSH SOLV ENTS. INGESTION: KEEP PERSON WARM & QUIET. GET
    MEDICAL ADVICE BEFORE TAKING FURTHER ACTION. ASPIRATION HAZARD.
    OBTAIN MEDICAL ATTENTION IN ALL CASES.

=====================  Fire Fighting Measures  =====================

Flash Point:-123F PROPELLANT

==================  Accidental Release Measures  ==================

Spill Release Procedures:EVACUATE AREA. USE SCBA RESPIRATOR. REMOVE
    IGNITION SOURCE. CONTAIN SPILL. PREVENT ENTRY INTO WATER SUPPLY.
    SUCTION INTO METAL CONTAINER. VENTILATE TO REMOVE RESIDUE VAPORS.
    MOP/SOAK UP W/INERT ABSORB ENT. REMOVE MOP/ABSORBENT TO OUTDOORS.

======================	Handling and Storage  ======================

Handling and Storage Precautions:DON'T PUNCTURE OR INCINERATE AEROSOL
    CANS. DON'T STICK PINS/NAILS/SHARP OBJECTS INTO OPENING ON TOP OF
    CAN. AVOID INHALATION OF VAPORS.
Other Precautions:AVOID CONTACT W/SKIN & EYES. DON'T SMOKE WHEN USING
    OR USE NEAR FLAMES. STORE IN COOL, DRY PLACE AWAY FROM LIKELY FIRE
    SOURCES. USE W/ADEQUATE VENTILATION. AVOID DELIBERATE INGESTION,
    WHICH IS HARMFUL . DON'T DROP CANS.
