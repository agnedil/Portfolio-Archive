=======================  First Aid Measures  =======================

First Aid:EYES: FLUSH W/LARGE QUANTITIES OF WATER FOR AT LEAST 15
    MINUTES. CONSULT MD IF IRRITATION PERSISTS. SKIN: REMOVE CLOTHING.
    WASH AREA W/WATER. INGEST: RINSE MOUTH W/WATER. DRINK LARGE
    QUANTITIES OF WAT ER/MILK. DO NOT USE ALCOHOL, BAKING SODA/ACIDIC
    ANTIDOTES. CALL MD/POISON CONTROL CENTER IMMEDIATELY. INHAL: REMOVE
    TO FRESH AIR. SUPPORT BREATHING (GIVE OXYGEN/ARTIFICIAL
    RESPIRATION) .

=====================  Fire Fighting Measures  =====================

Extinguishing Media:USE WATER OR CARBON DIOXIDE.
Fire Fighting Procedures:WEAR NIOSH/MSHA APPRVD SCBA & FULL PROT EQUIP
    . AVOID FUMES FROM SPILLED/EXPOSED LIQ. ACID CONTAM WILL PRDCE VERY
    IRRIT FUMES SIMILAR TO CHLORINE GAS.
Unusual Fire/Explosion Hazard:BLEACH DECOMPOSES WHEN HEATED. DECOMP
    PRODS MAY CAUSE CONTRS TO EXPLODE. A VIGOROUS REACTION IS POSSIBLE
    W/ORGANIC MATLS/OXIDIZING AGENTS & MAY RESULT IN FIRE.

==================  Accidental Release Measures  ==================

Spill Release Procedures:IF POSSIBLE, NEUTRALIZE WITH SODIUM SULFITE OR
    FERROUS SALT SOLUTION. THEN FLUSH WITH LARGE QUANTITIES OF WATER TO
    DILUTE. AVOID HEAT AND CONTAMINATION WITH ACID MATERIALS. DO NOT
    USE COMBUSTIBLE MATE RIALS SUCH AS SAWDUST TO ABSORB HYPOCHLORITE.
Neutralizing Agent:NONE SPECIFIED BY MANUFACTURER.
