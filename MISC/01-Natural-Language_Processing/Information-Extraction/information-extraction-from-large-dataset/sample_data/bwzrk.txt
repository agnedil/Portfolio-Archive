=======================  First Aid Measures  =======================

First Aid:INGESTION-RINSE MOUTH WITH WATER SEVERAL TIMES. SKIN-REMOVE
    COMPLETELY WITH A DRY PAPER CLOTH OR PAPER TOWEL, BEFORE WASHING
    WITH DETERGENT AND WATER. INHALATION-MOVE PERSON TO FRESH AIR.
    EYES-IMMEDIA TELY FLUSH EYES WITH PLENTY OF WATER FOR AT LEAST 15
    MIN AND GET MEDICAL ATTENTION IF IRRITATION PERSISTS.
