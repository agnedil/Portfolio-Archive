=======================  First Aid Measures  =======================

First Aid:SKIN: IN CASE OF CONTACT, IMMEDIATELY FUSH WITH PLENTY OF
    WATER FOR AT LEAST 15 MINUTES WHILE REMOVING CONTAMINATED CLOTHING
    AND SHOES. GET MEDICAL ATTENTION. EYES: WASH EYES WITH PLENTY OF
    WATER FOR  AT LEAST 15 MINUTES, LIFTING LOWER AND UPPER EYELIDS
    OCCASIONALLY. GET MEDICAL ATTENTION IMMEDIATELY. INHALATION: REMOVE
    TO FRESH AIR.  IF NOT BREATHING, GIVE ARTIFICIAL RESPIRATION. IF
    BREATHING IS D IFFICULT, GIVE OXYGEN. CALL A PHYSICIAN. INGESTION:
    GIVE SEVERAL GLASSES OF WATER OR MILK. VOMITING MAY OCCUR BUT DO
    NOT INDUCE. NEVER GIVE ANYTHING BY MOUTH TO AN UNCONSCIOUS PERSON.
    GET MEDICAL ATTE NTION IMMEDIATELY.
