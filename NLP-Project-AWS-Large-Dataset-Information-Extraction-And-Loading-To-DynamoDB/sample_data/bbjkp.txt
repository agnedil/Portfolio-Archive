=======================  First Aid Measures  =======================

First Aid:INHALATION: REMOVE TO FRESH AIR. IF NOT BREATHING, GIVE
    ARTIFICIAL RESPIRATION. IF BREATHING IS DIFFICULT, GIVE OXYGEN.
    INGESTION: ASPIRATION HAZARD. DON'T INDUCE VOMITING. IF SPONTANEOUS
    VOMITING OCC URS, HOLD HEAD LOWER THAN HIPS. SKIN/EYES: IMMEDIATELY
    FLUSH W/PLENTY OF WATER FOR AT LEAST 15 MINS. OBTAIN MEDICAL
    ATTENTION IN ALL CASES.

======================	Handling and Storage  ======================

Handling and Storage Precautions:ATTACKS SOME FORMS OF
    PLASTIC/RUBBER/COATING. STORE IN TIGHTLY CLOSED CONTAINERS IN
    COOL/DRY/WELL-VENTILATED AREA. AVOID EYE/SKIN/CLOTHING CONTACT.
Other Precautions:STORE IN NITROGEN ATMOSPHERE/OUTSIDE/DETACHED. INSIDE
    SHOULD BE IN STANDARD FLAMMABLE LIQUID STORAGE. BOND/GROUND
    CONTAINERS TO AVOID STATIC SPARKS. DON'T SMOKE. USE NON-SPARKING
    TOOLS. DON'T WEAR CON TACT LENSES. AVOID VAPOR INHALATION.
