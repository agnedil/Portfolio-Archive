Reports of Carcinogenicity:NTP:NO    IARC:NO	OSHA:NO
Health Hazards Acute and Chronic:ACUTE: INHALATION MAY CAUSE
    RESPIRATORY IRRITATION, HEADACHE, DIZZINESS, NAUSEA. CONTACT WITH
    SKIN AND EYES MAY CAUSE IRRITATION. INGESTION MAY CAUSE DIZZINESS,
    NAUSEA, VOMITING. CHRONIC: PROLONGED OR  REPEATED EXPOSURE MAY
    CAUSE SKIN SENSITIZATION OR DERMATITIS.
Explanation of Carcinogenicity:NO INGREDIENT OF A CONCENTRATION OF 0.1%
    OR GREATER IS LISTED AS A CARCINOGEN OR SUEPECTED CARCINOGEN.
Effects of Overexposure:INHALED-COUGHING, WHEEZING, SHORTNESS, OF
    BREATH, DIZZINESS, NAUSEA, VOMITING. INGESTED-DIZZINESS, NAUSEA,
    VOMITING. EYES-REDNESS, TEARING, BURNING SENSATION OR PAIN.
    SKIN-REDNESS, DISCOMFORT.
Medical Cond Aggravated by Exposure:SKIN DISORDERS, RESPIRATORY
    DISORDERS.

=======================  First Aid Measures  =======================

First Aid:EYES-FLUSH WITH WATER. CONTACT A PHYSICIAN IF IRRITATION
    PERSISTS. SKIN-WASH WITH SOAP & WATER. INHALED-REMOVE TO FRESH AIR.
    IF BREATHING DIFFICULT, ADMINISTER OXYGEN. INGESTED-DO NOT INDUCE
    VOMITING!  CONTACT A PHYSCICIAN. IF VOMITING OCCURS, KEEP HEAD
    BELOW KNEES TO PREVENT ASPIRATION.

======================	Handling and Storage  ======================

Handling and Storage Precautions:STORE IN A COOL, DRY PLACE AWAY FROM
    SOURCES OF IGNITION AND INCOMPATIBLE MATERIALS. KEEP OUT OF DIRECT
    SUNLIGHT. KEEP BELOW 120F.
Other Precautions:"EMPTY" CONTAINERS MAY RETAIN RESIDUE WHICH MAY BE
    DANGEROUS. DO NOT PRESSURIZE, CUT, WELD, BRAZE, SOLDER, DRILL,
    GRIND, OR EXPOSE SUCH CONTAINERS TO HEAT, SPARKS, FLAMES, STATIC
    ELECTRICITY OR OTHER  SOURCES OF IGNITION.
