=====================  Fire Fighting Measures  =====================

Flash Point:5F TCC.
Lower Limits:0.50
Extinguishing Media:CO*2,DRY CHEMICALS,FOAM,WATER FOG OR MIST.
Fire Fighting Procedures:USE WATER TO COOL EXPOSED CONTAINERS.
Unusual Fire/Explosion Hazard:A VAPOR ACCUMULATION WOULD FLASH AND/OR
    IF IGNITED.

==================  Accidental Release Measures  ==================

Spill Release Procedures:VENTILATE AREA,REMOVE SOURCE OF FLAME,SPARKS
    OR OTHER IGNITION HAZARD.CONTAIN SPILL & PUMP INTO DRUMS;OR SOAK UP
    IN ADSORBENT MEDIUM.FINALLY FLUSH SPILL AREA WITH COLD WATER.IF
    SPILL ENTERS SEWER NOTI FY SEWER AUTHORITIES.

======================	Handling and Storage  ======================

Handling and Storage Precautions:STORE AWAY FROM HEAT & STRONG
    OXIDIZING AGENTS.STORAGE TANKS SHOULD BE DIKED.PROTECT AGAINST
    STATIC CHARGES WHEN TRANSFERRING.
Other Precautions:USE ONLY WITH ADEQUATE VENTILATION.CONTAINS METHYL
    ALCOHOL CANNOT BE MADE NON POISONOUS.
