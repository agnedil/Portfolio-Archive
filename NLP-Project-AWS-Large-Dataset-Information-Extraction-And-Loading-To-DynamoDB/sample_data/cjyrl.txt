=======================  First Aid Measures  =======================

First Aid:EYES: IMMEDIATELY FLUSH WITH PLENTY OF WATER FOR AT LEAST 15
    MINUTES, OCCASIONALLY LIFTING UPPER AND LOWER LIDS. GET MEDICAL AID
    IMMEDIATELY. SKIN: GET MEDICAL AID. FLUSH WITH PLENTY OF SOAP AND
    WATER  FOR AT LEAST 15 MINUTES WHILE REMOVING CONTAMINATED CLOTHING
    AND SHOES. INGESTION: INDUCE VOMITING. IF CONSCIOUS AND ALERT, GIVE
    2-4 CUPFULS OF MILK OR WATER. NEVER GIVE ANYTHING BY MOUTH TO AN
    UNCON SCIOUS PERSON. GET MEDICAL AID. INHALATION: REMOVE TO FRESH
    AIR IMMEDIATELY. IF NOT BREATHING, GIVE ARTIFICIAL RESPIRATION. IF
    BREATHING IS DIFFICULT, GIVE OXYGEN. GET MEDICAL AID. NOTES TO
    PHYSICIAN:	TREAT SYMPTOMATICALLY & SUPPORTIVELY.
