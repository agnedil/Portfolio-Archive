=======================  First Aid Measures  =======================

First Aid:SKIN & EYES: FLUSH WITH WATER FOR 15 MINUTES. INHALATION:
    REMOVE PERSON FROM EXPOSURE. GIVE ARTIFICIAL RESPIRATION IF
    BREATHING STOPS. INGESTION: INDUCE VOMITING. NEVER GIVE ANYTHING BY
    MOUTH TO UNCON SCIOUS PERSON.

==================  Accidental Release Measures  ==================

Spill Release Procedures:DILUTE & WASH SPILLAGE WITH WATER. AVOID SKIN
    & EYE CONTACT & HIGH TEMPERATURES. WEAR RUBBER GLOVES DURING SPILL
    CLEAN-UP.

======================	Handling and Storage  ======================

Handling and Storage Precautions:MAINTAIN FLUX AT NORMAL ROOM TEMPS.
    USE FLUX AT TEMPS 1050-1600F. FLUX IS USED WITH BRAZING FILLER
    METALS CONTAINING CADMIUM. SEE OTHER PRECAUTIONS.
Other Precautions:CADMIUM OXIDE FUMES, TLV OF 0.5 MG/CUM, ARE A GREATER
    HAZARD THAN FLUORIDE FUMES FROM FLUX. ZINC OXIDE FUMES, TLV OF 5
    MG/CUM, MAY ALSO BE EMITTED FROM FILLER METAL BURNING BRAZING.
