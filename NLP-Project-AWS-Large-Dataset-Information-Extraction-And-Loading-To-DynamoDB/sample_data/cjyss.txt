    LOWERING EYELIDS OCCASIONALLY. GET MEDICAL ATTENTION. SKIN:
    THOROUGHLY WASH EXPOSED AREA FOR AT LEAST 15 MINUTES. REMOVE
    CONTAMINATED CLOTHING . LAUNDER CONTAMINATED CLOTHES BEFORE REUSE.
    IF IRRITATION DEVELOPS OR PERSISTS, GET MEDICAL ATTENTION.
    INGESTION: NOT A HAZARD UNDER NORMAL CONDITIONS OF USE. 
    INHALATION:  NOT A HAZARD UNDER NORMAL  CONDITIONS OF USE.
