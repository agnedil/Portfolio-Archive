=======================  First Aid Measures  =======================

First Aid:EYES: IMMED FLUSH WITH PLENTY OF WATER FOR AT LST 15 MINS,
    OCCASIONALLY LIFTING UPPER & LOWER LIDS. GET MED AID. SKIN: IMMED
    FLUSH WITH PLENTY OF SOAP & WATER FOR AT LST 15 MINS WHILE REMOVING
    CONTAM  CLOTHING & SHOES. GET MED AID IF IRRIT DEVELOPS OR
    PERSISTS. WASH CLOTHING BEFORE REUSE. INGEST: IF VICTIM IS
    CONSCIOUS & ALERT, GIVE 2-4 CUPFULS OF MILK OR WATER. NEVER GIVE
    ANYTHING BY MOUTH TO AN U NCONSCIOUS PERSON. GET MED AID IMMED.
    INHAL: REMOVE FROM EXPOS TO FRESH AIR IMMED. IF NOT BRTHG, GIVE
    ARTF RESP. IF BRTHG IS DFCLT, GIVE OXYG. GET MED AID IF COUGH OR
    OTHER SYMPTOMS APPEAR. NOTES TO M D: TREAT SYMPTOMATICALLY &
    SUPPORTIVELY.
