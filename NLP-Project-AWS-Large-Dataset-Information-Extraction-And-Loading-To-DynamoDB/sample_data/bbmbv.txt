=======================  First Aid Measures  =======================

First Aid:EYES/SKIN: FLUSH WITH WATER FOR 15 MINUTES. INGESTION: DON'T
    INDUCE VOMITING. GIVE A GLASS OF WATER. DON'T GIVE LARGE AMOUNTS OF
    WATER AS THIS MAY CAUSE VOMITING. INHALATION: REMOVE TO FRESH AIR.
    OBTA IN MEDICAL ATTENTION IN ALL CASES.

=====================  Fire Fighting Measures  =====================

Fire Fighting Procedures:COOL CANS WITH WATER IN VICINITY OF FIRE.
Unusual Fire/Explosion Hazard:AEROSOL CANS MAY RUPTURE OR BURST ON
    EXPOSURE TO TEMPS >120F. EXTREMELY FLAMMABLE.

    WATER AND DETERGENT.
