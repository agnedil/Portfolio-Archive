=======================  First Aid Measures  =======================

First Aid:INHALATION: IMMEDIATELY REMOVE TO FRESH AIR. KEEP PERSON
    CALM. GIVE CPR/OXYGEN IF NECESSARY. SKIN: FLUSH W/WATER. TREAT FOR
    FROSTBITE IF NEEDED. EYES: IMMEDIATELY FLUSH W/PLENTY OF COLD
    WATER. OBTAIN  MEDICAL ATTENTION IN ALL CASES.

=====================  Fire Fighting Measures  =====================

Flash Point:NONE
Extinguishing Media:FOAM, ALCOHOL FOAM, CO2, DRY CHEMICAL, WATER FOG

    SPILLS.

Handling and Storage Precautions:DON'T STORE >120F. DON'T STORE/USE

    PROFESSIONAL USE ONLY. TO BE USED ONLY BY THOSE PERSONS WHO HAVE
    BEEN PROPERLY TRAINED TO USE THIS PROD UCT & INSTRUCTED IN ITS SAFE
    HANDLING.
Other Precautions:DON'T BREATHE VAPORS. DON'T GET IN EYES/ON SKIN.
