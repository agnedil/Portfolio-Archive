First Aid:INHALED-REMOVE TO FRESH AIR. GET MEDICAL ATTENTION.
    EYES-FLUSH WITH LOTS OF WATER FOR 15 MINUTES. GET MEDICAL
    ATTENTION. SKIN-REMOVE CONTAMINATED CLOTHES. WASH WITH SOAP &
    WATER. INGESTED-GET IMMEDIAT E MEDICAL ATTENTION.

=====================  Fire Fighting Measures  =====================

Autoignition Temp:Autoignition Temp Text:340C
Extinguishing Media:WATER FOG.
Fire Fighting Procedures:MANTAIN SAFE QUANTITY-DISTANCE RELATIONSHIP TO
    PROTECT AGAINST SHRAPNEL AND PROPOGATION TO NEARBY EXPLOSIVE
    COMPONENTS.
Unusual Fire/Explosion Hazard:WHEN SUBJECTED TO SUITABLE IMPETUS, THIS
    UNIT WILL DETONATE CREATING A BLAST ACCOMPANIED BY SHRAPNEL AND A
    FIREBALL.

==================  Accidental Release Measures  ==================

Spill Release Procedures:LEAD AZIDE DECOMPOSES BY FLOODING WITH A 10%
    SOLUTION OS SODIUM HYDROXIDE, FORMING LEAD HYDROXIDE AND SODIUM
    AZIDE. ALL OTHER MATERIALS ARE DISPOSED OF IAW WASTE DISPOSAL
    INSTRUCTIONS BELOW.
Neutralizing Agent:10% SOLUTION OF SODIUM HYDROXIDE.

======================	Handling and Storage  ======================

Handling and Storage Precautions:STORE IN A COOL, DRY PLACE IN AN
    APPROVED MAGAZINE.
Other Precautions:DO NOT DROP OR STRIKE UNITS. PROTECT FROM STRAY
    ELECTRICAL SIGNALS, RF, AND STATIC. DO NOT REMOVE SHUNT PLUG PRIOR
    TO MATING WITH ELECTRICAL CONNECTOR. USE ONLY AN APPROVED SQUIBB
    TESTER BRIDGEWIRE RE SISTANCE CHECKS.
