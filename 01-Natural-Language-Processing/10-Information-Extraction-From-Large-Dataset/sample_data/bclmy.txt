=======================  First Aid Measures  =======================

First Aid:EYES: FLUSH IMMED WITH H*2O FOR 15 MINS. CALL MD. SKIN: WASH
    WITH WATER. CALL MD. INHAL: REMOVE TO FRESH AIR. RESTORE BREATHING.
    KEEP WARM & QUIET. CALL MD. INGEST: CALL MD. REMOVE CONTAMINATED
    CLOTHI NG.
