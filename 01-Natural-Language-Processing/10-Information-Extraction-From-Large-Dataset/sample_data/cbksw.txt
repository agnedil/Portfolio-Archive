=======================  First Aid Measures  =======================

First Aid:INGEST:CALL MD IMMEDIATELY . INHAL:REMOVE TO FRESH AIR.
    SUPPORT BREATHING (GIVE O*2/ARTF RESP) . EYES:IRRIGATE IMMEDIATELY
    & REPEATEDLY W/WATER FOR AT LEAST 15 MINUTES & GET PROMPT MEDICAL 
    ATTENTION. SKIN:WASH EXPOSED AREAS W/SOAP & WATER.

======================	Handling and Storage  ======================

Handling and Storage Precautions:KEEP DRY UNTIL USED.
Other Precautions:DO NOT BREATHE CEMENT DUST; PROTECT SKIN, EYES, NOSE
    FROM DUST. PRECAUTIONS MUST BE OBSERVED BECAUSE CEMENT CAN BURN
    W/OUT WARNING: LITTLE HEAT IS SENSED.

    LEVELS.
