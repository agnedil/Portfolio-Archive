    SKIN.IT HAS BEEN KNOWN TO CAUSE MILD CENTRAL NERVOUS SYSTEM

=======================  First Aid Measures  =======================

First Aid:SKIN:WASH WITH SOAP AND WATER.CONSULT A DOCTOR IF IRRITATION
    CONTINUES.INHALATION:CONSULT PHYSICIAN IF IRRITATION OF RESPIRATORY
    PASSAGE OCCURS.EYE CONTACT:FLUSH THOROUGHLY WITH WATER FOR 15 MIN.
    CONS ULT PHYSICIAN IF IRRITATION CONTINUES.INGESTION:IF PERSON IS
    CONSCIOUS,GIVE WATER OR MILK TO DILUTE STOMACH CONTENTS.CONSULT
    PHYSICIAN
