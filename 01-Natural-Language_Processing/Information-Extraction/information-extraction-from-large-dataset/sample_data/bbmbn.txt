    CITRUS JUICE OR 1 OZ OF VINEGAR IN WATER. FOLLOW W/MILK OR OLIVE
    OIL. INHALATION: REMOVE TO FRESH AIR. OBTAIN MEDICAL ATTENTION IN
    ALL CASES.
