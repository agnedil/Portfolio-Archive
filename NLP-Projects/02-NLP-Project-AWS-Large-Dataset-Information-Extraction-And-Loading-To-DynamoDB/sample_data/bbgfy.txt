First Aid:EYES: FLUSH IMMEDIATELY WITH FRESH WATER FOR 15 MINUTES, GET
    MEDICAL ATTN. SKIN: IMMEDIATELY WASH WITH SOAP & WATER, GET MEDICAL
    ATTN IMMEDIATELY. INGESTION: INDUCE VOMITING, GET MEDICAL ATTN
    IMMEDIAT ELY. GIVE WATER & FOLLOW WITH DEMULCENTS.
