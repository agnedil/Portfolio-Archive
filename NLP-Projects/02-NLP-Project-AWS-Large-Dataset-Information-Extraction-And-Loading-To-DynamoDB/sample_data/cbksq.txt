=======================  First Aid Measures  =======================

First Aid:EYE: FLUSH W/WATER FOR AT LEAST 15 MINS OCCASIONALLY LIFTING
    UPPER & LOWER EYELIDS. SKIN: REMOVE CONTAMD CLTHG, THEN FLUSH
    W/WATER FOR AT LEAST 15 MINS. WASH CLTHG THORO BEFORE REUSE. INHAL:
    MOVE TO F RESH AIR. INGEST: DO NOT INDUCE VOMITING. IMMED OBTAIN
    MED HELP. SEND TO HOSPITAL. ONLY IF PATIENT CONSCIOUS, GIVE LARGE
    QTYS OF WATER/MILK. IF IRRIT CONTINUES, GET MED ATTN IMMED.
